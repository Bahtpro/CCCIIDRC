ABOUT CCCII.DRC

CCCII Has An International Reach In The World

The Congolaise Chamber of Commerce, International and Industry was established in 2020. The chamber is mandated to protect and develop the interests of the business community through business networking, advocacy, arbitration and capacity building.

As an international business membership and global trade support institution, CCCII.DRC links commercial and industrial interests globally while supporting SMEs, corporates, and multinational enterprises.
